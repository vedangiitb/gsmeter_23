GS Meter

A platform to track the progress made by the General Secretaries of IIT Bombay towards their manifesto points.

Overview

The GS meter is a platform through which every student of IIT Bombay can stay up-to-date with the progress made by the General Secretaries for the points on their manifestos.

The aim is to increase accountability by maintaining a public platform that tracks the progress of the elected representatives throughout the year.

How to use the GS meter

  • The meaning of symbols is specified at the top of each page.
  • You can click on ongoing/incomplete/unclear manifesto points for further elaboration if one has been provided by the concerned General Secretary.
  • At the end of each page, there is also a section for points that were not present on the manifesto but have been completed, if provided by the concerned General Secretary.
  • The pie chart at the top of each page displays the overall progress each General Secretary has made with regard to their manifesto.

Disclaimer

The GS meter is continuously updated. A point marked as incomplete/ongoing could be marked complete later in the tenure. Feel free to reach out to us via our social media handles or webmail for any clarifications.
